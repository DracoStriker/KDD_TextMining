Eleven-year-old Jake Chambers experiences visions involving a Man inBlack who seeks to destroy a Tower and bring ruin to the world, and aGunslinger who opposes him. Jake's mother, stepfather, andpsychiatrists dismiss these as dreams resulting from the trauma of hisfather's death the previous year.At his apartment home in New York City, a group of workers from analleged psychiatric facility offer to rehabilitate Jake; recognizingthem from his visions as monsters wearing human skin, he flees. Jaketracks down an abandoned house from one of his visions, discovers ahigh-tech portal, and travels to a post-apocalyptic world calledMid-World.
