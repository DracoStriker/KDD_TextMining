Please go see The Big Sick. It's a wholesome and original romanticcomedy that will tug at your heartstrings, make you smile and verylikely generate a high respect for comedian Kumail Nanjiani and hiswife Emily V. Gordon, who wrote the film together. It is inspired bythe first time Kumail and Emily met and the distressing events thatfollowed when Emily endured a major sickness, forcing Kumail to faceher parents and become torn between his family's cultural expectationsand his feelings for Emily. The Big Sick explores the ups and downs ofinterracial relationships in an effortlessly funny and genuinelypoignant manner, backed up by raw and endearing performances.
