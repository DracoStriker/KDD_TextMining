In 1989, before the collapse of the Berlin Wall, MI6 agent JamesGascoigne is shot and killed by KGB agent Yuri Bakhtin, who steals theList, a piece of microfilm concealed in a wristwatch that contains thenames of every active field agent in the Soviet Union. Ten days later,Lorraine Broughton, a top-level spy for MI6, is brought in to beinterrogated by MI6 executive Eric Gray and CIA agent Emmett Kurzfeldabout her mission to Berlin.
