Inspiring and emotionally charged story about a young boy namedAuggie (the incredible Tremblay) with a congenital facial deformity whoenters public school for the first time after being home schooled byhis loving parents (Roberts and Wilson, both solid) and facing the realworld as well. Based on the best seller by RJ Palacio, filmmakerStephen Chbosky - who co-adapted with Steve Conrad and Jack Thorne -gets some truly wonderful performances from his young actors overalland balances the treacle with heartfelt warmth, compassion and neverforgetting the theme - Just Be Kind.
