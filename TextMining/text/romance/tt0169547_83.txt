In the footsteps of David Lynch, this film explores the nightmareunderbelly of suburbia USA, when ordinary people try to beextraordinary. With dire results. Beautifully crafted, and with adefinitive performance by Kevin Spacey, ably supported by a great cast,this is a standout film. A man in middle age crisis, feeling himself tobe a loser in every respect, becomes infatuated with a young girlfriend of his daughter. Revives his zest for living, but complicationsinclude a homophobic neighbor, with a weird son; his wife having arelationship with the Real Estate King, and his daughter becominginvolved with the weirdo next door. An explosive and unexpected finishmakes this a film to remember.
